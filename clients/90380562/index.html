Where do I find my employee number?
You can find your employee number on your staff oyster card.

I want a paper survey?
We haven't issued any paper surveys this year. Viewpoint is only available online. We have sent you an email with a unique link. If you can't find this we can reissue or you can use the generic link https://surveys.enginetransformation.com/s3/Viewpoint2021

Will you ask me for my name or employee number, or any other information that could identify me individually?
For those completing the survey using a generic link or QR code (this means that you will not be completing the survey following a link you have received in your email), you will need to enter you employee number to access the survey. The reason why you have to enter your employee number is so that ENGINE is able to identify what part of organisation you sit in. No responses will be linked directly to you and when it comes to reporting, no group below 10 responses will be reported on.
